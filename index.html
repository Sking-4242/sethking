Who Am I

Seth King

Cybersecurity student, cloud infrastructure builder, and creator of Archon — an open-source, AI-assisted cloud architecture design tool. I like building things that make complex infrastructure easier to see, secure, and reason about.

I'm currently studying Cybersecurity (with a minor in Cloud Computing) at Brigham Young University - Idaho, graduating April 2027. Alongside coursework, I work as a Teaching Assistant, tutoring and grading students in SQL, Linux fundamentals, and introductory cloud technologies.

Most of my time outside class goes into Archon, a visual cloud architecture design tool I'm building that spans AWS, Azure, GCP, and on-prem environments — combining a drag-and-drop canvas, AI-powered Terraform generation, live cost estimation, and automated security/compliance validation. Before pivoting toward security and cloud, I led sales teams at Solar Pros, co-leading a team to $720K in revenue and running weekly sales training.

I'm drawn to the intersection of security and infrastructure: things like SIEM-driven detection, segmented network design, and the practical work of making cloud environments both usable and defensible.
